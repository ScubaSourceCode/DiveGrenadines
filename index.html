About The Owner: Glenroy N Adams

Setting the highest
standards for diving in the Grenadines
For Over 35 Years

Glenroy Adams Born and brought up in Bequia, the sea has been apart of his life since birth, He pursued becoming an instructor in Florida and became the first Vincentian to become a licenced Scuba Diving Instructor. He as tallied over 8,000 Dives to date. Passionate about the environment Glenroy is always doing his part to keep the reefs and the beaches clean to preserve the pristine beauty that is the Grenadines. St.Vincent and the Grenadines is lucky to have him here.
